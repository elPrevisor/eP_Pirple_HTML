MY FAVORITE FOOD

Special Chinese rice

ingredients

  • vegetable oil
  • rice
  • 1 chicken breast
  • 100 grams of beeft
  • 100 grams of pork
  • 100 grams of white shrimp
  • Vegetables: onion, carrot, pepper, sweet pepper and chives
  • sEnglish sauce
  • soy sauce
  • garlic sauce
  • sweet sauce/li>
  • Salt
  • seasonings

Preparation

  1. 2 cups of rice
  2. clean and chop vegetables into small pieces
  3. clean and chop the meat into small pieces
  4. clean and chop the pork into small pieces
  5. clean and chop the shrimp into small pieces
  6. sauté the meat for 5 minutes
  7. sauté fry the pork for 5 minutes
  8. sauté the shrimp for 5 minutes
  9. mix all vegetables, meat, pork, shrimp and saute
  10. add sauces seasonings
  11. after 10 minutes serve the dishes
